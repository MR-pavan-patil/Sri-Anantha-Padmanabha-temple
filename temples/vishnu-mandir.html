Vishnu Mandir

A beautiful ancient shrine dedicated to Lord Vishnu, nestled deep within the forest clearing.

Temple Details

History & Significance

Vishnu Mandir is the first major stop on the Sacred Trail, located approximately 3 kilometers from the main temple complex. Set inside a peaceful natural clearing of the dense Bidar forest, this ancient stone temple has stood for centuries as a place of quiet contemplation and intense devotion.

The temple features intricate stone pillars and wall carvings illustrating various incarnations (avatars) of Lord Vishnu, with a special emphasis on the Dashavatara themes. Devotees visit this shrine to experience the powerful, calming spiritual energy that flows through the natural surroundings.

Every Saturday, the temple sees a large gathering of devotees who come for the special Abhishekam and Vishnu Sahasranama Archana rituals, which are conducted by the resident priests starting from early morning.
